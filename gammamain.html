3D Carousel

Gamma Γ

In this single player action-packed, unconventional sandbox shooter, you play as an infiltration and sabotage unit equipped with a powerful weapon capable of hijacking mechanical enemies and vehicles.

From agile combat drones to heavily armoured tanks and gunships use the tools at your disposal to turn the tide of battle.

Core Mechanic

Mechanical characters and vehicles can be hijacked and controlled which requires energy, the player has an energy reserve which automatically recharges during the day.

At night however the player needs to find energy or light sources that they can recharge from. The players shield uses energy when activated.

Upgrade Mechanic

The player has a upgrade system that uses ingame currency that can be earned by exploring the world and hidden caches. Upgrade examples include:

-Shield

The player gets a shield for protection but uses energy when activated by projectiles or explosives

-Speed

Player can move faster while crawling

-Flight

Flight time is increased allowing the player to stay vertical longer

-Damage

Player receives less damage

Enviroments

Environments will vary heavily between desert, bushland, tropical, snow and urban. Additionally we want to feature space and Luna environments.

Supported Platforms

Primarily we are going to release on PC through Steam but are developing for consoles in mind like Xbox, PlayStation and the Nintendo Switch

Development Timeline

We have the backbone of the game mostly complete and are actively working on the demo to attract players. When the funding is secured we expect a 1, 2 year development cycle. Most of the work after the demo will be art assets and additional features for some levels. We will not be using generative AI for art assets in any capacity.
