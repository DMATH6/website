Gamma Γ (TBA Title)

Set after the extinction of humanity, you awake into a dead world with a new body and an objective you don’t yet understand.

In this single player action-packed, unconventional sandbox shooter, you play as an infiltration and sabotage unit equipped with a powerful weapon capable of hijacking mechanical enemies and vehicles. From agile combat drones to heavily armoured tanks and gunships use the tools at your disposial to turn the tide of battle.

Core Mechanic

Mechanical characters and vehicles can be hijacked and controlled which requires energy, the player has an energy reserve which automaticly recharges during the day. At night however the player needs to find energy or light sources that they can recharge from. The players shield uses energy when activated.

Upgrade Mechanic

The player has a upgrade system that uses ingame currency that can be earned by exploring the world and hidden caches. Upgrade examples include:



-Shield

The player gets a shield for protection but uses energy when activtaed by projectiles or explosives



-Speed

Player can move faster while crawining



-Flight

Flight time is increased allowing the player to stay verticle longer



-Damage

Player recives less damage



Enviroments

Enviroments will vary heavily between desret, bushland, tropical, snow and urban. Additonaly we want to feature space and luna enviroments.

Supported Platforms

Primariliy we are going to release on PC through Steam but are developing for consoles in mind like Xbox, Playstation and the Nitendo Switch

Development Timeline

We have the backbone of the game mostly complete and are activily working on the demo to attract investors. When the funding is secured we expect a 1, 2 year development cycle. Most of the work after the demo will be art assets and additonal features for some levels. We will not be useing generative AI for art assets in any capacity.
