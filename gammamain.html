Overview

Set after the extinction of humanity, you awake into a dead world with a new body and an objective you don’t yet understand.

Gamma Γ

In this single-player action-packed, unconventional sandbox shooter, you play as an infiltration and sabotage unit equipped with a powerful weapon capable of hijacking mechanical enemies and vehicles.

From agile combat drones to heavily armoured tanks and gunships, use the tools at your disposal to turn the tide of battle.

Core Mechanic

Mechanical characters and vehicles can be hijacked and controlled which requires energy. The player has an energy reserve which automatically recharges during the day.

At night, however, the player needs to find energy or light sources that they can recharge from. The player's shield uses energy when activated.

Upgrade Mechanic

The player has an upgrade system that uses in-game currency earned by exploring the world and hidden caches.

  • Shield: Protection that uses energy when hit by projectiles or explosives.
  • Speed: Player moves faster while crawling.
  • Flight: Flight time is increased for vertical movement.
  • Damage: Player receives less damage.

Environments

Environments vary between desert, bushland, tropical, snow, and urban. Space and Luna environments are planned.

Supported Platforms

Primarily PC via Steam, with consoles in mind such as Xbox, PlayStation, and Nintendo Switch.

Development Timeline

The backbone of the game is mostly complete, with active development on the demo to attract players. After funding, we expect a 1-2 year development cycle focusing on art assets and additional features. No generative AI will be used for art assets.
